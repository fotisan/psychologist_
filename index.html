FAQ

How long are sessions, and how often do we meet?

Most sessions are 50 minutes. Many clients start weekly, then adjust as things feel steadier.

Do you offer video sessions?

Yes. Secure video sessions are available for California based clients, when appropriate for your needs.

What is your style as a therapist?

Warm and direct. We stay compassionate, and we keep things practical, so therapy translates into real life.
